About Us

Reid Repairs was originally opened in a small shop just down the street in January of 2016. We're very proud to have taken over our new location in the center of Hatboro. Tom McMackin trusted us to take over his shop and we aim to uphold their standard of quality and timely work. Our goal is to provide the high quality and consistent automotive repair that you would expect from a large dealership along with the personal relationship of a small shop.
